Hi, I'm Vishak.

I'm a web developer with a background in mechanical and environmental engineering.

In college, I studied environmental engineering, and was introduced to C, MATLAB, and Python. I found programming to be my favorite subject- not only for the satisfaction of solving a complex problem, but because of the significance of data science and computing in the future of engineering. I knew that if I wanted to be at the forefront of innovation, I needed to be at the forefront of the technology that was impacting it the most.

I decided to commit to a career shift, introduced myself to Python, and enrolled in a full-stack web development bootcamp to learn Ruby, SQL, JavaScript, and the skills essential to being a good developer.

Since then, I've built multiple fully functional applications utilizing Rails and Backbone, from project management apps inspired by Trello, to my latest project, 'Sitbit'- a health visualization app built on Backbone and Vega.js. I'm currently seeking opportunities in the Bay Area where I can work on challenging projects and continue to grow as a developer.
